Caramel Apple

Description

How to make a caramel apple

Ingredients

  • green apple
  • wooden stick
  • hot drizzly caramel in all its glory
  • cooking paper

Steps

  1. Shove a small wooden stick into the core of an apple, making sure that enough stick is still outside of the apple so as to serve as a handle.
  2. Holding the stick, smoother the apple in the hot caramel until the apple is completely and thoroughly covered, then place the apple stick-up on a small piece of cooking paper.
  3. Let the caramel apple cool for about 10 minutes before eating.
